Economist

Description: Economists typically research and analyze economic issues and design policies or make recommendations for solving economic problems. They often study historical trends and use the information to make forecasts. Many economists work for corporations and help them understand how the economy will affect their business.

  • Education Required: A masters or PhD in economics
  • Training (What you need to learn): Be able to perform complex research, problem solving, good communication, and write in an understandable manner
  • Experience (Any job experience prior?): 21 semester hours in economics and 3 hours of statistics
  • Annual Pay: $100,000
  • Job Trend: 6% increase between 2016-2026
  • Threatened by AI?: Economists deal a lot with interacting with people and presenting information to people in a clear, concise way. AI would not be able to comprehend the differences between what humans might understand so the job of economists does not seem to be too threatened by AI. However, some fields that deal with strictly numbers, could be in danger of being replaced.
