Real Estate Agent

Description: Real estate agents buy and sell property to and from people. Their job requires them to watch the available inventory in order to secure the best deal for a potential customer, and also to persuade people to buy whatever it is they're selling. Real estate agents have to be able to use math in order to determine what the best price is to sell to a customer, depending on the amount of days the house has been on the market, how badly the customer wants the house, and the current market competition.

  • Education Required: high school diploma or equivalent
  • Training (What you need to learn): to get license, you take an exam and pass it
  • Experience (Any job experience prior?): social skills and enthusiasm are looked into when hiring applicants
  • Annual Pay: $44,090
  • Job Trend: 2.6% employment
  • Threatened by AI?: Ai won't be able to threaten this job because you need good social skills. Real estate agents are hired because of how they talk to people. An ai might not have any social skills.
