Investment Analyst

Description: An investment analyst provides stock brokers, fund managers, and stock market traders with financial information and counsel based on global investment data.

  • Education Required: Education Required: Bachelor's degree for a junior position, and a Graduate degree for any further position.
  • Training (What you need to learn): Trainee analysts are required to complete prescribed professional qualifications, depending on the organisation and assigned role.
  • Experience (Any job experience prior?): Experience researching, collecting and analyzing information.
  • Annual Pay: $81,058
  • Job Trend: 11% increase (2016-2026)
  • Threatened by AI?: An investment analyst provides stockbrokers, fund managers, and stock market traders with financial information, advice and recommendations. AI can give financial information more quickly and efficiently.
