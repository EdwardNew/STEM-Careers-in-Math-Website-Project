Math Teacher

Description: Math teachers are important figures of society in helping to develop children's interests in the world. They teach how to think logically and use critical thinking to solve problems, which are useful skills to develop in life.

  • Education Required: Bachelor's degree in education for middle and high school teacher. Must be licensed by its state to teach.
  • Doctoral degree for post secondary teachers(college level).
  • Training (What you need to learn): Other Advanced Courses with topics related to classroom management and teaching methods.
  • Experience (Any job experience prior?): Internships: working under the supervision of a licensed teacher.
  • Pay: Median annual wage of $58,000
  • Job Trend: 6% growth in math teaching expected between 2014-2024. 16% increase expected for college teachers between 2014-2024
