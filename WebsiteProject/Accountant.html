Accountant

Description: An accountant records financial data, typically for a company. They also issue financial statements to assure that the company is financially stable and has enough money to keep the company running. Accountants also send out sales records to customers. Overall accountants keep track of the money and record the data to assure the company's success.

  • Education Required: Undergraduate degree in accounting
  • Training (What you need to learn): Algebra and geometry courses
  • Experience (Any job experience prior?): 2 to 5 years of experience in accounting to get a certificate
  • Pay: Median annual wage of $69,350
  • Job Trend: 10% growth in accounting positions expected between 2016-2026
  • Threatened by AI?: rather than eliminating accountants, humans will have new colleagues - machines
