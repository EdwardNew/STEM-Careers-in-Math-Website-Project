Software Engineer

Description: A software engineer is someone who is engaged in computer software developments and applies the principles in software creations. They develop many things such as operating systems, software structure, and even video games. They bring the mathematics into computer programming.

  • Education Required: Bachelor's degree in computer science.
  • Training (What you need to learn): Programming languages such as JAVA, C#, etc.
  • Experience (Any job experience prior?): The ability to determine the needs of the software user or objective of the software
  • Annual Pay: $102,880
  • Job Trend: 24% increase (2016-2026)
  • Emerging: Software engineering is a career that is projected to grow greatly because of the high demands in computer software that will soon be needed.
  • Threatened by AI?: AI would never replace software engineers, but they would be used to aid the software engineers in order for them to work more efficiently.
