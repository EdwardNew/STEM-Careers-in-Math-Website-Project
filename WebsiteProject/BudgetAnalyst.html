Budget Analyst

Description: Budget analysts help direct the supply of funds in the company. They develop and execute budgets, as well as help to calculate future finances and costs of different needs the business will require.

  • Education Required: Bachelor's degree in math related fields
  • Training (What you need to learn): Different courses in accounting, economics, and statistics are recommended
  • Experience (Any job experience prior?): Internships with other budget analysts for applying what's learned
  • Annual Pay: $75,000
  • Job Trend: 7% in between 2016-2026
  • Threatened by AI?: This job could be threatened by AI in the future due to how reliant it is on math. Most of the job relates to doing complicated calculations and figuring out budgets for the company. However, with every job, critical thinking is needed to see which situation would be the best for the company, and AI is unable to do that.
