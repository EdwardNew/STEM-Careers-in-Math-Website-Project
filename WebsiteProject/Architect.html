Architect

Description: An Architect designs buildings, and supervises the construction to make sure everything is carried out properly. Their job is very important because if the structure of the building is flawed, the whole building could collapse. They use math in order to make sure the weight will be properly supported and to make sure that the construct is safe.

  • Education Required: B.S. in Architecture & Master of Architecture/Bachelor of Architecture
  • Training (What you need to learn): train under licensed architect and earn a total of 700 internships unit.
  • Experience (Any job experience prior?): Internship and certification needed
  • Annual Pay: $76,100
  • Job Trend: 7% growth from 2014-2024
  • Threatened by AI?: Possibly. An AI might be able to design a safer structure more efficiently than a human can.
