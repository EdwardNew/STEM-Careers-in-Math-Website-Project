Cost Estimator

Description: A cost estimator collects and analyzes data in order to estimate the time it takes and the money to build a structure and create a plan for the estimated results. They estimate the cost of most projects based on previous information and projects. They usually specialize in a certain industry or product.

  • Education Required: Bachelor's degree in mathematics, engineering, construction science or construction management.
  • Training (What you need to learn): Manage cost estimating process throughout project life cycle
  • Experience (Any job experience prior?): Experience producing successful estimates
  • Annual Pay: $60,390
  • Job Trend: 11% increase (2016-2026)
  • Threatened by AI?: A cost estimator analyzes data in order to estimate the time and money it takes to build a structure. An AI would be able to complete this task faster than humans.
